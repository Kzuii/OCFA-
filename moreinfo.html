About the OCFA

The Official Collegiate Football Association (OCFA) is dedicated to creating a realistic and immersive Roblox football experience for players of all ages. We strive to provide organized leagues, robust team management, and a community-driven atmosphere where student athletes can showcase their skills and have fun.

What We Offer

  • Organized Seasons & Tournaments: Weekly matchups, playoff brackets, and championships.
  • Player Progression & Stats: Track your stats and improve over time.
  • Active Community: Discord, social media, and in-game events keep you connected.
  • Coaching & Team Management: Become a head coach, draft players, and lead your team to victory.

Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer to Roblox football, OCFA welcomes you. Jump in, meet new friends, and experience the thrill of competitive play!
